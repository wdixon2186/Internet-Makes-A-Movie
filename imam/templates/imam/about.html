Internet Makes A Movie!

Internet Makes A Movie is a site that's main focus is collaboration among fellow creatives. The idea is to put forth a story idea and allow anyone to submit a maximum of 5 pages to our site. From there we will allow other users to cast one vote on their favorite. (They can't vote for themselves.) The winner has their pages added to the script and a new round starts the following week. The goal is to get everyone writing and hopefully end with a full length screenplay, that is coherent and impressive. After the screenplay is finished the contributors will be contacted about production, while the next screenplay idea will start the following week. Once production begins each contributor will be given a team of actors as well as a small stipend to produce their contribution. After they finish, we hire an editor to put the film together and have a completed feature length film.
